XPROAX

local eXplanations for text classification with PROgressive neighborhood ApproXimation

XPROAX falls into the category of local explanation method and is specified for text classifiers.

XPROAX provides more detailed explanations than other explanation methods, which consist of four components: (i) intrinsic words, (ii) extrinsic words, (iii) factuals, (iv) counterfactuals.

Both qualitative and quantitative evaluations show the effectiveness and stability of XPROAX in comparison to state-of-the-art methods.
